Back Street

I don’t remember why I started to like photography, but I can tell you that the first time I went outside to shoot street photography I was full of expectations and adrenaline.

Street photography is about real people with their feelings, thoughts and behaviors, without filters. They live in free will.

Magnum is the oldest and largest cooperative of photographers. I learned from them a new way of understanding photography.

This section is about how people can express their feelings without showing their faces.
