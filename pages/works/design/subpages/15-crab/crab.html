#15 F.S.M. (Crab ver.)

why

The F.S.M. (Flying Spaghetti Monster) is the God of a religion which in reality is a social movement that promotes a light-hearted view of religion and originated in opposition to the teaching of intelligent design in public schools.
As it would be really messy to put spaghetti on this page, I decided to make him a crab dress first.

the making of ‚Üì
where

For a certain period of time I had been doing pixel art drawings that only took me one or two days each. Still, one day I decided to undertake a bigger project (spending more than a month).

I wanted to set it in the 1990s to recreate some of the anime films that I usually watched as a kid.

Arale and Dr. Slump are two really weird and peculiar characters who made a touching impression on me.

Later on I realized that I like them because they are gray characters, meaning they are neither bad nor good people, they look just like real people and cause the same sensation as the ‚ÄúSo Real‚Äù song of Jeff Buckley.

what

In this project I let my imagination fly. I wondered how I could display my feelings about the Anime in a single image, what would be the best way to express them?

I spent more than 160 hours just drawing on Aseprite, my Pixel Art tool.

On top of that I need to mention the time I spent searching for scenes and inspiration, as well as the long sessions I devoted to learning the history of manga and the process of drawing it.

Although I originally drew many characters for this project, in the end I selected only some of them to be displayed.
