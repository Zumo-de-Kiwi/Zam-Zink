#06 Comb

why

The comb is the item that gets closest to our epicenter, our brain.

The brain is the source of our thoughts. If we have a place to save our most private thoughts and value memories, it is a safe called the brain. The said safe is safe simply because it is understandable.

where

It all started with the ideation of my website, having seen that many personal websites place their logo on the top left corner of the screen.

The vast majority of authors use their initials to design their logo, but I prefer a shape rather than a combination of fonts: the simpler the better.

what

This is the first personal logo I made.

I chose a very versatile logo that gets well with different colors, looks nice in different sizes and is even suitable for animations, like the ones displayed during page loading.

Step 1: making the logo shape.
Step 2: logo color
  • Implementing some color combinations with famous brands, to see how it looks
  • After the first implementation of colors I make some minor adjustments in the logo.
  • Then I implement Material Design colors on the logo.
Step 3: Displaying the logo on my business card and on my website.
