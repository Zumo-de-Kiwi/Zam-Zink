#19 Chilli

why

In my opinion, one of the funniest elements of this game are the bugs, the more visual and bizarre the better. Having this in mind, here I chose a chilli and a banana, two completely different types of food, which in my game would have totally opposite functions or utilities.

where

My friend Erik was doing a Master's degree in Unity, a game engine software, and he ended up requesting me to design some characters as well as some keys for a keyboard for the game tutorial.

what

Here are some of tThe characters I made for Erik’s game. They have some core movements: standby, walk, attack and be attacked. On top of those, I added a few more for every character.Incidentally, as they have x and y-axis, they need to have four positions for each movement.

UMBRELLA MONSTER

KAPPA MONSTER

Many of the movements comes of my mind with some childhood memories with videogames.

Like some characters of Animal Crossing, when they wake up, their eyes have a plate size.
The exaggerated teary eyes of some enemies being attacked in the game Maple Story.

THE KEYBOARD OF THE GAME

The keyboard is far easier than the characters, only on or off, pressed or no pressed.
