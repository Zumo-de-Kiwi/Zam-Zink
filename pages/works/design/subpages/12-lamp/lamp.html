#12 Lamp

why

To me Luxo Jr. is like an animal. There are certainly many animals and characters whose head you would use as a logo.

where

Now, in the era of Kawaii cats and big eyes, it is difficult to find characters that express their emotions with their actions and not through their facial movements.

what

After Disney bought Pixar, some people feared that their freedom would be restricted, whereas others believed Disney would let them maintain the same degree of freedom. My theory is that virtually Mickey is under the power of Luxo Jr., who physically owns Disney. Consequently, no matter whether Disney has control over Pixar or not, at the end of the day decisions stay at home.
