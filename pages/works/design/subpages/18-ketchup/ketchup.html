why

52 Hertz is a Taiwanese movie, named after an enigmatic whale that produces a 52-hertz sound, much higher pitched than other whales. It is believed to be the only whale in the world that produces that pitch. That fact reminds me of many bright minds that are ahead in time, those who invent or promote ideas or objects that others will consider to be bound to be a total failure.

One of these people is Steve Jobs, who introduced the computer to ordinary people. One of his more emblematic computers is definitely the Macintosh 128K.
