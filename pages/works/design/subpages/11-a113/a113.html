#11 A113

why

Because this is a work done in Calarts, I was wondering what insignia item to put related to Calarts or Disney, who founded it.

This ended up to be the most difficult item to come out with. I tried more than four items but none of them were good enough. It seemed that I was on the wrong path. I then realized that what I needed to find first was not an item but a code, specifically A113. This secret code in Pixar corresponded to a room where the most brilliant minds of Pixar were working.

After having found out the magical code, I easily came out with a poker card, whose inscribed number was as impossible as when Pixar managed to create the first animation movie that was computer-generated . They are really ahead still nowadays.

What I like best is how they pay attention to little details and take their time to research a specific topic, go to that particular country, analyze the people's behaviors and social life, the architecture, draw the textures of their streets and even consider how the lighting affects the streets.
