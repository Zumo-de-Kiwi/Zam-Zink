#04 Pen

why

Originally, I was trying to design an eraser, but as its most common shape is rectangular, it doesn't get well with the other elements in the Gundam set, so I decided to design a pen instead, as the antagonist of the eraser.

When it comes with pencils, I found that the Palomino Blackwing has a design that stands out, its eraser is replaceable and with a rectangular form that enables more detailed erasing.

where

MUJI was becoming popular when I was in secondary school, it was really different from the average stationery shop. Muji shops have more classy lighting, a coffee-like vibe, and look more woody and cozy.

Muji has an item that to me stands out: its black eraser. I was really confused at first: why is it black? Doesn't it look dirtier being black? Now I know that what they achieve is the opposite: it does look cleaner because the eraser will not turn darker after erasing. Therefore, the color of the eraser will always look brand new.
